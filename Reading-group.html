Reading Group

Reading Group is a group meeting which is participated by Lina's group (Data Dynamic) members. Its main purpose is to share the paper-reading experience, enhance the research communication among group members and improve presentation skills.

  1. The reading group meetings will be held on Monday every two weeks.
  2. Every reading group will have only one presenter which is roundly selected (slides are required), generally everybody would present roughly at least 2 times a year.
  3. Selecting the best papers from the top conferences of your research interests. Please note that the paper MUST come from a CORE A* conference with less than 2 years old (i.e., it is published this year or last year, no older).
  4. every reading group will last for about one hour which includes two stages: presentation (25 mins) and discussion(35 mins).

Please feel free to contact me if you have any inquiries or suggestions on Reading Group.

Time: 3:00pm - 4:00pm Monday per fortnight
Venue: K17, 401K
